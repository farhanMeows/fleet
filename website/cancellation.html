Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

1. Cancel anytime

There is no lock-in. You can stop using fleetdeck whenever you like — email contact@fleetdeck.in from your registered address and we will close the paid service on your account within 2 business days.

2. What happens when you cancel

  • Billing stops: no further usage invoices are issued after the cancellation takes effect. Usage already delivered up to that point remains payable.
  • Current period: paid features remain available until the end of the period already invoiced; nothing is clawed back.
  • Credits: an unused prepaid balance stays usable if you return, or can be refunded under the Refund Policy (7-day window for unused credits).
  • Local tool: the open-source fleet CLI keeps working locally — cancellation only affects paid, account-linked features.

3. Unpaid invoices and payment links

An invoice you have not paid can simply be left unpaid — payment links carry no obligation until captured. Ask us and we will cancel the link and void the invoice.

4. Account deletion

Cancelling does not delete your account. If you also want your data removed, say so in the same email — deletion is handled as described in the Privacy Policy.
