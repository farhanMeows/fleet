Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

1. The short version

The fleet tool is local-first: your agents' events, transcripts, and project data stay in SQLite on your machine (~/.fleet/) and are never sent to us. What we hold is the minimum needed to run accounts and billing: your Google account basics and your invoice and payment records.

2. What we collect

  • Account: when you sign in with Google we receive your name, email address, and Google account ID, and we record sign-in counts and timestamps.
  • Billing: invoices we issue to you (amounts, line items, status) and payment references from Razorpay (order, payment, and payment-link IDs). Card, UPI, and bank details are handled entirely by Razorpay — we never see or store them.
  • Website: your sign-in session is kept in your browser's localStorage. We do not run advertising trackers or analytics cookies.
  • Software: the fleet CLI sends no telemetry. Your code, prompts, and agent activity never leave your machine unless you enable a cloud feature that explicitly says so.

3. How we use it

To authenticate downloads, issue and settle invoices, provide support, notify you about releases and account matters, and satisfy tax and accounting obligations (including GST invoicing). We do not sell or rent personal data.

4. Who we share it with

Only service providers needed to operate: Google (sign-in), Razorpay (payments), Vercel (hosting), and our database provider (Aiven). Each receives only what its function requires. We may disclose information if legally compelled.

5. Retention and deletion

Account data is kept while your account is active. Invoice and payment records are kept as long as tax law requires. To delete your account and associated personal data (except records we must retain), email contact@fleetdeck.in from your registered address — we will confirm within 7 days.

6. Security

Data in transit is encrypted with TLS. Download access is authorised with signed, expiring tokens. Payment collection happens on Razorpay's PCI-DSS-compliant infrastructure.

7. Changes and contact

We may update this policy; the date above reflects the current version. Questions or requests: contact@fleetdeck.in.
