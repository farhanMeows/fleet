Refund Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

1. Unused credits — 7-day window

Credit top-ups can be refunded in full within 7 days of payment provided the balance is unused. If part of the balance has been consumed, the remaining unused portion is refundable within the same window.

2. Usage already delivered

Charges for usage that has already been delivered — completed agent sessions, monitored projects, synced tokens on a settled usage invoice — are non-refundable, except where the service materially failed to work and our support could not resolve it.

3. Duplicate or erroneous payments

Duplicate payments, payments captured after a failed checkout, or amounts charged in excess of the invoice are refunded in full — no time window applies. If your bank debits money but the payment never reaches Razorpay, the bank reverses it automatically, typically within 5–7 business days.

4. How to request a refund

Email contact@fleetdeck.in from your registered address with the invoice number (e.g. FD-2026-0001) and the payment reference if you have it. We respond within 3 business days.

5. How refunds are paid

Approved refunds are issued through Razorpay to the original payment method. Razorpay initiates the refund immediately on our instruction; banks typically credit it within 5–7 business days (up to 10 for some card issuers). Refunds are made in INR for the amount originally captured; we do not adjust for exchange-rate movement.
